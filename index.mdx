HCEGuard is a Windows desktop application that brings secure, rolling-code authentication to Halo CE servers. Players register once, then use a constantly changing OTP (one-time password) to sign in — eliminating unauthorized access and giving server administrators reliable identity control.

## How HCEGuard works

HCEGuard links your player identity to a secure account. When you sign in, the app verifies your OTP code against the server, generates local integration files for Halo CE, and monitors your session in real time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Register at the HCEGuard portal to get your player account and credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install and launch HCEGuard">
    Download the application, install it on Windows, and open it before starting Halo CE.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in with your OTP">
    Enter your nickname and the 6-digit OTP from your authenticator app or email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Halo CE">
    With HCEGuard running and authenticated, open Halo CE to play on protected servers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Always launch HCEGuard **before** opening Halo CE. Starting HCEGuard while Halo CE is already running will prevent the integration from activating correctly.
</Note>
