---
title: "Send Feedback and Report Issues in HCEGuard"
description: "Use the HCEGuard feedback form to report bugs, suggest improvements, or flag security concerns. Requires an active signed-in session."
---

HCEGuard includes a built-in feedback form that lets you send comments, bug reports, and suggestions directly to the development team. All submissions go to a private queue reviewed by the team — they are not posted publicly. You must be signed in to submit feedback.

## Open the feedback form

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to HCEGuard">
    Open HCEGuard and sign in with your Nick and OTP code. The **Send Feedback** option only appears in the menu when you have an active session.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open the menu">
    Click the **≡** menu button in the top-left corner of the app.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Send Feedback">
    Click **Send Feedback** in the dropdown menu. The feedback form opens as a separate window.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Fill out the feedback form

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a category">
    Select the category that best describes your feedback from the **Category** dropdown:

    | Category | When to use it |
    |---|---|
    | **Bug or issue** | Something is broken or behaving unexpectedly. |
    | **Suggestion** | An idea for a new feature or improvement. |
    | **Performance** | The app is slow, freezing, or using too many resources. |
    | **User experience** | The interface is confusing or difficult to use. |
    | **Security** | You have found a potential security vulnerability or concern. |
    | **Other** | Anything that does not fit the categories above. |
  </Step>

  <Step title="Write your message">
    Type your feedback in the **Message** field. Be as specific as possible — describe what you were doing, what you expected to happen, and what actually happened. For bug reports, include any error messages you saw.

    The message field is required. You cannot submit without entering text.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Submit your feedback">
    Click **Send** to submit the form. HCEGuard will confirm that your feedback was received and close the form automatically. If something goes wrong during submission, an error message will appear — check your internet connection and try again.

    Click **Cancel** at any time to discard your feedback and close the form without sending.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your feedback is submitted privately to the HCEGuard team. You will not receive an automatic reply, but the team reviews all submissions.
</Note>
