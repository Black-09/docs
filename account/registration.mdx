Before you can sign in to HCEGuard and access authenticated Halo CE servers, you need a player account. Registration takes place on the HCEGuard website and only needs to be done once. After your account is approved, you use a one-time password (OTP) every time you sign in.

## How to register

1. Open HCEGuard on your Windows PC.
2. Click the **Register** button on the login screen. HCEGuard will open the registration page in your default web browser.
3. Fill in the registration form on the website and submit it.
4. Once your account is created, you will receive your OTP credentials, which you use to sign in from the app.

If HCEGuard cannot open the registration link automatically, a message will appear letting you know. In that case, visit [hceguard.site](https://hceguard.site) directly and navigate to the registration page.

## Nickname requirements

Your nickname is your permanent in-game identity on HCEGuard-protected servers. When choosing one, keep the following rules in mind:

- **Alphanumeric only** — letters (A–Z, a–z) and digits (0–9) are allowed. Spaces, symbols, and special characters are not.
- **Maximum 11 characters** — nicknames longer than 11 characters will be rejected.

Choose your nickname carefully. It is the name other players and server administrators will see.

## After you register

Once you submit the registration form, your account enters a review process. During review, HCEGuard verifies your account details before granting access. You do not need to take any further action — the review happens automatically.

When your account is approved, you will be able to sign in using your nickname and a 6-digit OTP code. See [Understanding OTP Codes](/account/otp) for details on how OTP authentication works.

## Account review

The review process may take up to 24 hours. If you attempt to sign in before your account is approved, HCEGuard will display the following message:

> Your account is under review. This process may take up to 24 hours.

If you see this message, wait and try again later. You do not need to re-register.

If your account has been suspended, you will see a different message indicating the suspension. Contact the HCEGuard support team if you believe your account was suspended in error.
